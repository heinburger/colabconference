Colab Conference

We are excited to announce CoLab’s 2014 Conference will be happening the weekend of June 21-221. The purpose of this conference is to generate a public forum around what ideas and issues we all take to be important, and foment collaboration toward progressing them. During the weekend we hope to give people a chance to share ideas, come up with plans of action, and participate in a bit of manual labor.

The location of the conference is at our land - 656 Fairview School Lane, Gainesboro, TN. In order to RSVP, click below and join our google group, after which we’ll contact you with further information. If you have any questions feel free to get in touch with us at contactcolab@googlegroups.com

Hope to see you in June!
